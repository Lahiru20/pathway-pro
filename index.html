Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform about?

Our platform helps software engineers prepare for technical interviews through AI-driven mock interviews, practice questions, and an integrated coding environment with real-time feedback from PathwayBot.

What languages are currently supported?

We support Java and Python. More languages will be added soon to provide a diverse coding practice experience

What is PathwayBot?

PathwayBot is your personal AI assistant that provides real-time feedback on your code during practice interviews. It helps improve your solutions by suggesting optimizations and identifying potential errors.

Can I practice without an AI-driven interview?

Yes! You can access a wide range of practice questions in our dedicated Practice Questions section and solve them at your own pace.

Is this platform suitable for beginners?

Absolutely. Our platform is designed to cater to software engineers at all levels, from beginners to experienced professionals preparing for advanced technical interviews.

How can I provide feedback or request new features?

We value your feedback! You can share your suggestions and requests through the Contact section available on our platform.
